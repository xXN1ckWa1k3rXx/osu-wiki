# Standard/Mania Weekend End of Autumn Tournament

![SWEAT banner](img/banner.jpg)

<!-- TODO: Link to Organisations page when it exists -->

The **Standard/Mania Weekend End of Autumn Tournament** (***SWEAT***) was an osu! and osu!mania BYOC LAN tournament hosted by osu! Riverside. It took place during *Highlander Con Fall 2019*, a gaming festival hosted by Highlander Gaming at the University of California, Riverside (*UCR*).

## Event details

SWEAT was a LAN event, meaning it happened in-person. osu! Riverside had a room reserved for the duration of Highlander Con, and it was a friendly place for all rhythm gamers to hang out, in addition to hosting this tournament.

Details about Highlander Con Fall 2019 were posted on its [Eventbrite page](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/highlander-con-fall-2019-tickets-74580744013):

- Location: Highlander Union Building (HUB), 900 University Ave, Riverside, CA 92507
- Time: Sunday, November 17, 2019 starting at 11:00AM

## Prizes

All prizes were awarded to osu! and osu!mania winners separately.

## Ruleset

### Stages

This is a double-elimination tournament.

#### osu!

- **Seeding:** Players will play through all the maps in the mappool and will given a certain amount of points depending on how well they place against other players.
- **Round of 16:** Best of 5
- **Quarterfinals:** Best of 5
- **Semifinals:** Best of 7
- **Finals:** Best of 9
- **Grand finals:** Best of 11

#### osu!mania 4K

- **Seeding:** Players will be seeded by rank. There will be no group stage.
- **Semifinals:** Best of 7
- **Finals:** Best of 9
- **Grand finals:** Best of 11

### Matches

- Scores will be in ScoreV2.
- Matches will be Head to Head.
- Failed scores will count.
- Invites will be sent out 5-10 minutes before the scheduled time.
- There will be no warmup maps.
- `!roll` will determine picks and ban order. The higher roll will pick the the first map in map pool. The lower roll will choose to ban first.
- Bans will be declared before picks.
- Bans and picks will be in alternating order.
- In the case where both players are one game off from a win, a predetermined tiebreaker map will be played.
- In the Grand Finals, the participant from the winner's bracket will have a 1-point advantage.

#### osu!

- Players have one ban in all bracket stages.
- Players may pick Hidden or Flashlight for a DoubleTime map.
- In a FreeMod pick, both players must use HardRock, Hidden, Flashlight, Easy, or a combination of any.
- Players are allowed to use mods on Tiebreaker but is not required.
- Players cannot pick the same mod twice in a row.

#### osu!mania 4K

- In stages prior to Grand Finals, players have one ban. In Grand Finals, they have two.
- All charts will include FreeMod; no mods are required, but mods may be added if the player would like. Allowed mods are Hidden, FadeIn, Flashlight, and Mirror.
- If all players agree, Grand Finals may use the same mappool as Finals. In that case, players will only have one ban.
